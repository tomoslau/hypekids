Celebrate Spring Break With Our Food-Themed Appaman Editorial

With a little help from some ice cream.

Fashion By Emily Jensen / 36 Mins ago

Spring is underway and that means it’s time to break out the shorts and dresses and the ice cream. To welcome the new season we teamed up with kids’ brand Appaman on an editorial using the label’s Spring/Summer 2018 collection. And since the line features so many food-themed items like adorable hamburger and ice cream shirts, what better place to shoot it then at old school soda shop Brooklyn Farmacy?

To go with the spring threads you’ll also of course need some new sneakers, and nothing goes better with a banana dress than the Vans‘ colorful Glitter Fruit Sk8-Hi and Slip-On. And for the pineapple shirt, you of course need some sneakers from the SpongeBob Squarepants x Vans collection, in honor of SpongeBob‘s pineapple house under the sea. Top off the look with colored sunnies from children’s eyewear brand Sons + Daughters and your kids are ready for spring break.

Wearing an ice cream shirt calls of course for an ice cream cone, and there’s no better place for all things sweet than Brooklyn Farmacy. Situated in Carroll Gardens, the soda fountain has been in existence since the 1920s – first as an actual working pharmacy before its revival as an ice cream and soda shop in 2010. The Farmacy is the perfect stop for classic New York grub like pastrami sandwiches and egg creams, but for those summer Instagram shots be sure to order the Pink Poodle, made with hibiscus soda and vanilla ice cream. And like our kid models, save room for a whoopie pie (or two).

Head over to Appaman to shop the latest collection and for limited time, get 20% off with the code HYPE20. Be sure to take the kids on a visit to Brooklyn Farmacy to get a taste of those ice cream float, and for more spring apparel, see the latest arrivals at HBX Kids.

Brooklyn Farmacy
513 Henry Street
Brooklyn, NY 11231
